    "\n",

    "\n",

    "\n",
    "\n",
    "and the equivalent matrix form\n",
    "\n",
    "$$A \\cdot x = b$$\n",
    "\n",
    "where $A$ has shape `(n,m)`, the unknown $x$ has shape `(m,1)`, and $b$ has shape `(n,1)`."
